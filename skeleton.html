    For a Halloween engineering project, the challenge was to create attractions for a Halloween fair. I then decided to create a skelleton robot that would dance to Michael Jackson's "Thriller". The robot had a precoded dance routine and started when the viewer pushed a button on the back. Then, the song would start and the robot preformed its moves.
